Kn Restaurent was started in the year 2021 by the Kn Group of Restaurants. Named after the world’s most precious spice, Kn restaurant serves freshest ingredients with the finest taste. Our chefs fuse together flavor and aroma to capture the essence of the traditional South Indian & North Indian cuisine.

Kn Restaurent has become synonymous with Authentic Vegetarian Barbecue, Non Vegetarian Barbecue & North Indian Cuisine. The Kn Restaurent chain epitomizes the royal experience in flavour, aroma and purity. It was first of its kind as it introduced the unique “ do it yourself “ concept in dining. Kn’s dining experience comes complete with a grill at the centre of the table and variety of starters skewered and ready for the guests to marinate according to taste and then grill or roast to perfection. This customized dining has been getting a stupendous response from connoisseurs of good food. In fact Kn Restaurent has established a stellar customer base that speaks of its popularity.
